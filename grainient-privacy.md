# grainient Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Grainient handles information when you use the app.

Grainient lets you take a photo or choose one from your photo library. Selected images are processed locally on your device to extract colors and generate gradients. The app does not upload photos for processing and does not use a backend service for image generation.

Grainient does not require user accounts, and it does not ask you to create a profile to use the app.

If you choose to share a generated image, sharing happens through the standard iOS share sheet and is controlled by the sharing option you select.

You retain all rights to the photos and content you use with Grainient.

If you have privacy questions about Grainient, contact [talakhadzeani@gmail.com](mailto:talakhadzeani@gmail.com).
